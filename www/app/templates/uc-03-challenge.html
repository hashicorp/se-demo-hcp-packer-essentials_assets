Maintaining auditability with an image registry

Challenges Consequences
Image sprawl: Image diversity across multiple platforms leads to an extensive collection of assets. Teams that do not track their image assets centrally contribute to inconsistency, higher costs and security exposures.
Unmanaged lifecycle: Teams that create images need to manage the lifecycle of images in legacy, active and dormant stages. When a team does not follow given standards, technical, security, and governance guidelines place an organization in vulnerable situations.
