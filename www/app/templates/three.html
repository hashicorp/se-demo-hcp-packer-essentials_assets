What is Packer?

HashiCorp Packer

Packer is an Open Source configuration and templatization software tool that helps create machine images for multiple platforms.

Teams that use Packer consume machine images as appliances or golden images to deliver content faster, consistently and securely.

Organizations that use Packer benefit from the standardization of desired states for machine images, leading to shorter delivery cycles and faster employee onboarding.
