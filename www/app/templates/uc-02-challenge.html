What is HCP Packer?

Definition Use Cases
HCP Packer is a cloud service that bridges the gap between image creation and deployment with image-managment workflows. In essense, it is a registry that tracks your machine and container images' metatdata. HCP Packer can be used to keep track of your golden images. Tracking the metadata of your images enables development and security teams to create, manage, and consume images in a collaborative environment.
