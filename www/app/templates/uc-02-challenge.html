Use images in automated provisioning workflows

Challenges Consequences
Platform diversity: Provisioning workflows can vary depending on the target Cloud platforms, VMware or Kubernetes environments. Different workflow processes across multiple target environments introduce significant overhead for development, release and maintenance.
Asset sprawl: Image diversity across multiple platforms requires control to prevent loss and unintended exposure. Multiple teams need to manually maintain a ledger of assets in the development, release, and maintenance stages. There is little control over assets' lifecycle, leading to forgetfulness, misplacement, and abandonment.
