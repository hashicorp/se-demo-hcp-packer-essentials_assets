What is HCP Packer?

HCP Packer

HCP Packer is a Cloud service that offers a cross cloud, central registry for machine images generated with Packer.

Teams can orchestrate the lifecycle of a machine image through its inception, updated iterations, and a controlled retirement.

The tacit significance is the introduction of modern automation workflows to reduce the overhead of creating, managing and releasing machine images across multiple platforms.
