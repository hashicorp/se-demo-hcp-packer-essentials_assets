Summary of benefits

Approach Benefits
Approve images: With corporate guidance, teams can automate the promotion of machine images to support emerging standards. Teams can plan for emerging improvements to their machine images and promote them using modern DevOps automation patterns from development to pre-production to production stages.
Maintain iterations: When modifications are identified, teams can quickly upstream and downstream changes with controlled automation. Machine images can iterate through revisions as needed, and consuming teams can rely on acquiring currently authorized versions in their application domain.
Retire images: Teams can plan to retire machine images that no longer meet current standards. Teams can plan to retire machine images within a grace period and sometimes immediately to avoid vulnerabilities.
